Tater Tot Cassarole

Tater Tot Cassarol

A midwestern classic. This dish is full of flavor.

There are many variations of this great dish. This one is the go to for our family

Ingredients

  • Tater tots
  • Ground beef
  • Cream of chicken soup
  • Corn
  • Green beans
  • (Optional) Shredded Cheese

Steps

  1. Pre heat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Brown your ground beef in a skillet with generous amounts of pepper and salt.
  3. When the beef is cooked through add cream of chicken soup
  4. After the soup fully combines with the ground beef, add both the corn and the green beans. Letting the pan simmer on medium heat until veggies are cooked.
  5. Transfer meat and veggie mix to a baking dish. Spread evenly and top with tater tots.
  6. Place pan into pre heated oven and let cook until tots are crispy.
  7. At this point you can add the optional cheese to the top of the dish. In our family cheese is a MUST!
  8. Enjoy!
