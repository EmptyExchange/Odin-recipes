Fettuccine Alfredo

This recipe is a major hit with the family. It is quick, easy, and DELICIOUS!

With only a few easy steps and a little love, this recipe will be sure to make it to your menu to be made many times!

Ingredients

  • Fettuccini noodle
  • Butter
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Heavy whipping cream
  • Garlic
  • Assorted household spices

Steps

  1. First things first. Get your water boiling for your noodles. This is generally the most time consuming part of the whole recipe.
  2. While your noodles boil heat a sauce pan and add your butter and garlic.
  3. When the butter has melted, slowly add the heavy whipping cream.
  4. Let the sauce cook on medium high heat until the cream is warm.
  5. Add parmesan to the warmed cream. It is easier to add small amounts at a time, letting the previous amount melt before adding more.
  6. Stop adding cheese when the sauce is at a thickness that suits you. If the sauce is too thin add more cheese, if the sauce is too thick add more cream.
  7. While the sauce is cooking and combining flavors, add pepper to taste. The cheese adds a fairly large amount of salt to the dish. Be sure to give your sauce a taste and add additional flavors as desired.
  8. Drain your noodles and return them to your pot. Cover with the sauce and stir thoroughly.
  9. ENJOY!!!
